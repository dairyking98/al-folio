Welcome to my engineering blog and portfolio!

This site is built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/), a static site generator. I'll be using this space to:

- Share engineering projects and technical insights
- Document my learning journey
- Showcase portfolio work
- Write about topics that interest me

You'll find posts covering:

- **Software Engineering**: Development practices, tools, and techniques
- **Projects**: Detailed write-ups of personal and professional projects
- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides and how-tos
- **Thoughts**: Reflections on technology and engineering

## Getting Started

Feel free to explore the site using the navigation menu. You can:

- Browse posts by [tags](/blog/tag/)
